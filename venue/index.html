Symposium Venue

BalticSTE 2023 will be held in Šiauliai county Povilas Višinskis public library (ŠCPL).

ŠCPL is located in a modern, contemporary building. The library premises are suitable for visitors of all ages and needs, including those with various disabilities. Here operate various departments such as the Department of Regional Studies, Old Prints and Digitization, the Book Graphics Center with a unique Ex Libris fund of Gerardas Bagdonavičius, the Children and Youth Literature Department with an excellent toy library, the American Corner storing the largest number of publications, films and more in English in the region.
