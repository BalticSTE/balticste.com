14-17 June, 2027

7th INTERNATIONAL BALTIC SYMPOSIUM ON SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION

„SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY EDUCATION: BUILDING SUSTAINABLE FUTURES“

Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of the organizing committee, we are delighted to welcome you to Šiauliai, Lithuania, for the VIIth International Baltic Symposium on Science and Technology Education, BalticSTE 2027. The Symposium will be held in Šiauliai (Lithuania) in June 2027 during days 14-17. We cordially encourage you to attend and contribute to one of the major events of 2027 in the field of science and technology education. We are confident that you will appreciate the scientific program and the city of Šiauliai.

We look forward to seeing you in 2027 in Lithuania.

Kind regards,
Symposium committee

Activity timeline (no later than)

  • 30/SEP 2026 First announcement (call for papers)
  • 30/MAR 2027 Second announcement
  • 30/MAR 2027 Confirmation of reviewers
  • 15/APR 2027 Submission of papers for review
  • 30/APR 2027 All review feedback to authors
  • 20/MAY 2027 On-line registration closed
  • 20/MAY 2027 Submission of re-worked papers
  • 30/MAY 2027 Payment of registration fees by all participants (*See note below)
  • 25/MAY 2027 Provisional programme released to participants
  • 05/JUN 2027 Final programme released to participants and the public
Note* If final arrangements for payment of registration fees are not processed by 30th May 2027, paper(s) of those concerned, will NOT be included in the symposium programme. Kindly adhere to deadline dates. ‘Early is good’.
