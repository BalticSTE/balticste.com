Ways to reach Šiauliai City

  • Travel to Riga International Airport (RIX) and continue by Airport Express bus operated by Ollex. The bus goes from the airport directly to Šiauliai (120km.).
    Early online ticket booking is available.

    Check additional possibilities on Lux Express and Flix Bus.

  • Travel to Vilnius International Airport (VNO) and then to Šiauliai by bus or train (recommended).
    Book bus tickets or train tickets online.

    Additional possibilities: ECOLINES & Eurolines & Lux Express.

  • Travel to Kaunas international airport (KUN) and then to Šiauliai by bus (approx. 150 km.).
    Book bus tickets online.

  • Travel to Palanga international airport (PLQ) and then to Šiauliai by bus (approx. 150 km.).
    Book bus tickets online.

    For more possibilities see Rome2rio offers.

  • You can also plan your trip via our tourist agency West Express.
