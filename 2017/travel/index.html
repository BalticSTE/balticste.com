Travel & Accommodation

All travel and accommodation arrangements are the responsibility of participants. Participants are responsible for booking and commitments made with the hotels. Conference organisers take no responsibility for any commitments participants make with hotels in Šiauliai.

Our recommendations:

  • Hotel Šaulys
    Offering free Wi-Fi and a sauna, this centrally located hotel is just a 1-minute walk from Šiauliai’s pedestrian area. Guests enjoy free use of the gym, and Šiauliai Cathedral is a 3-minute walk away.

  • Hotel Šiauliai
    The hotel is situated in the centre of Šiauliai city which is the most convenient place for the guests visiting the town. Moreover, there are railway and bus stations near the hotel as well as the most important cultural institutions and banks.

  • Hotel Turnė
    This hotel in central Šiauliai is just 1 km from Šiauliai Station. All of its rooms feature satellite TV, a mini bar and a private bathroom. Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms.

Try booking early to guarantee a room.

Ways to reach Šiauliai City

  • Riga International Airport
    Travel to Riga International Airport and continue by the airport express bus or taxi to Riga Bus station. From the station to Siauliai. There is approx. 120 km from Riga to Siauliai. It takes approx. 2 hours by bus.

  • Vilnius International Airport
    Travel to Vilnius International Airport and continue by the airport bus or taxi to Vilnius Bus station. From the bus station to Siauliai. There is approx. 225 km from Vilnius to Siauliai. It takes approx. 3 hours by bus.

  • Palanga International Airport
    Travel to Palanga International Airport and continue by the airport express bus or taxi to Palanga Bus station. From the station to Siauliai. There is approx. 140 km from Palanga to Siauliai. It takes approx. 2.30 hours by bus.

  • Ecolines
    ECOLINES is the largest and most experienced coach operator in the Baltic States, which offers comfortable travel to 21 countries and about 200 cities on a daily basis.
