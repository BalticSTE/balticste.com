Important notes

This form is simply an application to attend. Once payment is made, you'll be accepted as a fully registered participant.
The participation fee will cover coffee breaks, welcome reception, symposium programme, symposium dinner, symposium materials, cultural programme.
Confirmation of payment should be sent to the organization committee via e-mail: balticste@gmail.com
Cancellations should be made in writing to the BalticSTE 2021 Secretariat. Cancellations will not be possible after May 20, 2021.
For any cancellation submitted till 20/05/2021, full refund will apply except from a processing fee of 25€ for Participants, 10€ for Accompanying Persons and of 15€ for Students. Bank charges to be deducted from the refunded amount.
